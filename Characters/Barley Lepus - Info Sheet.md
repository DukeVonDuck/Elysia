## Backstory
Barley was born and raised in [[Rabbithole]]. As his [[Lepus Family|family]] were the local priests of the [[12th Hero]] he grew up with fear and caution of the surrounding [[Widow's Briar]]. The young lad learned quickly how dangerous pride and arrogance can be. The young lads mind was frustrated by the adults always telling him to endure, to be patient and to be cautious. The urge to be free drove him to "adventure" into the woods. 
Everything was new and exciting until it wasn't. Barley was an easy prey for any predator and soon he found himself running for his life. Only fear and desperation filled his mind and the panic sat in as the way forward was blocked. Not ready for death but unavoidable the eyes closed. The sharp pain which he anticipated never came as he heard a voice calling out for him to run. Light returned and there he was. [[Barleys Father]] - a small and meek goblin like Barley himself - standing before him - protecting his most important person - only holding a dagger and his scriptures. 
He tried to run as he was told - he tried to follow what could have been the last request of [[Barleys Father|his father]] - his legs didn't move - it was his fault and now someone else had to pay the price. This moment of hesitation led to something impossible. Barley witnessed the desperate fight between the [[Barleys Father|man who raised]] him and the beast who preyed on him. It was brutal - it was gore - his stomach turned - but in the end the small goblin stood victorious. Relief washed over both of them as they reunited with tears streaming down their cheeks. On their way back [[Barleys Father|his hero]] didn't scold him. His [[Barleys Father|father]] talked about the teachings of the [[12th Hero]]. The fear he felt during this encounter showed him what was important and what had to be done. Fear can be hindering, a source of caution but it can also be a motivation to overcome whatever the source of it is. Barley finally understood why [[Rabbithole]] was worshipping the [[12th Hero]] in this moment.
The rest of his childhood the young priests son studied the teachings and helped out in the temple. He lived and breathed it. His faith would never waver after witnessing the tenants in such extremes during the encounter with the beast. Barley grew up into a splendid adult and priest - but his heart was broken. The old texts within the village told a different story about the [[12th Hero]]. The hero is a coward who betrayed the expectations and even sacrificed his own kin. Barley didn't want to believe it - he experienced what fear could accomplish. The priest pondered and pondered for years. The result? He had to preach the real teachings of the [[12th Hero]].
The revelation of his goal shocked all of [[Rabbithole]]. The young promising priest wants to leave the village and face the dangers of the outside world? The commotion was reigned in by Barleys parents. They knew Barley and saw in his eyes that nothing could weaken his resolve. The preparation for this pilgrimage started immediately and were lengthy. After many months Barleys first destination was decided - [[Elysia]].
The departure was a mixed bag of fear, anticipation, curiosity and hope. The journey was treacherous. Many dangers lurked along the way but Barley was prepared. He had his skills, knowledge and the teachings of the [[12th Hero]] to keep him safe. A month later the beaten exhausted priest reached his destination.
Barley soon started his preaching and sermons. He hoped he could plant the seed for his religion to only be utterly disappointed by [[Elysia]]. Everyone sneered and shunned him. [[The Church]] didn't appreciate people who went against THEIR grain. So Barley had to hide to save his own life once again and finds himself now in the [[Elysia#Underground|Underground of Elysia]]. 
# Rabbithole
Before the [[Manaplaque]] Rabbithole was a big metropolitan bustling with the chaos of everyday life. This organized chaos was swallowed up by the mutation of the flora around it. Tales tell about a forest which sprang out of the ground over night - the [[Widow's Briar]]. Nothing could stop it. No street, no buildings and no human. The forest reclaimed it's domain like a predator and it also changed the people. Many of the citizens suddenly started to evolve/mutate into something different. And what should remain after this chaos were only those who could adapt. These people embraced the teachings of the [[12th Hero]] to save their lives. Resisting was futile as even modern weaponry couldn't oppose the [[Widow's Briar]]. So they hid in fear. Days turned into weeks, weeks into months and the survivors started to gather in the safest places withing [[Widow's Briar]]. They built their new haven deep within the undergrowth. To remind all generations what saved them they named the city Rabbithole in reference to the symbolic rabbit of the [[12th Hero]]. Rabbithole is possible the only place with a deep faith in the [[12th Hero]].
