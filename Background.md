# Primer

So, this setting is essentially very much inspired by real world Earth, but under the premise that Myth and Magic were/are actually real.

Our story begins in the year 1451 Post-Sacrificare (After the Sacrifice/ATS), and is mostly set in the city of Elysia, which is very much a cyberpunk hellhole unless you got the moneys. More on that later.

# Geography
The world, which is referred to by its inhabitants as just "The World", is mostly comprised of one Super-Continent which makes up 40% of the total landmass and is called Gaia or also Central Earth. The other 4 almost-continents could be described as very large islands and they are literally referred to as the Northern, Southern, Western and Eastern Earth.

# Religion
So, an important point is this. Before the Sacrifice was made, the world was plagued by monsters and demons. And humanity only became safe when a girl ventured down into the deepest depths of Gaia. Some claim she and her companions killed a four headed black dragon and after that, the Nameless one was sacrificed. Others say she went down there alone and gave herself willingly to the Dragon which appeased the creature and all monsters.

So, essentially there are multiple differing religions, however there are two constants. A Nameless Girl and a Four-Headed Black Dragon.

I want to lean very strongly into the concept that even if some parts of a Mythology may be true, pretty much any religion is 95% fanfiction that was written around it.

Anyway. This underground megastructure is also known as Dragons Rest and it is true that The Nameless is sealed down there. Still watching over humanity. Even if the exact details are murky. And on top of Dragons Rest a temple was built which later evolved into Elysia.

So, essentially, it's like this city in current times is Night City, but also The Vatican at the same time.

The dominant religion wrote 12 heroes into the story though. 11 died in battle against the dragon. They all basically each represent a different Virtue/Commandment and are treated as saints. And the 12th, the hero who embodies cowardice is the one who gave up and sold his daughter's life to the dragon in exchange for his own life. In this version of the story, the dragon was appeased by the purity of The Nameless which led to it sparing both the Daughters life and that of all of humanity. However the coward-warrior was executed a hundred years later.

It is important that, to most, these stories are about as real as Christianity in the real world. Some are faithful, some are not. But there are ruins under Elysia. That much nobody can deny.

# Magic
The most important difference between our real world and The World of Gaia is that Magic is real. This is not something debatable as the existence of The Nameless One or a Four Headed Black Dragon. To use Magic, one has to channel an ambient force that exists within all of The World, dubbed Mana. The only restriction to this is that Magic requires an Astral Body to function and an Astral Body can only be exhibited by organic creatures. Thus, fully robotic life forms or AI are incapable of utilizing it. However, hybrid lifeforms that were born with an Astral Body do not lose or weaken it just by modifying the body. (No Shadowrun style Essence Loss here). This means that AI-Puppets (humans under strict neural influence of an AI via brainchip) can indeed use magic and are a somewhat clumsy workaround for this issue.

In more recent times it has been found that the actual channeling of Mana occurs via a cystic organ that attaches itself to the human brain and is thus called a Mana-Cyst. Some are born with one, but it can also be implanted and stimulated by exposing oneself to high quantities of Mana. However, while the Mana-Cyst has been confirmed to be the core facilitator, it still seems to require an Astral Body. This is essentially the limit of how far science has gotten in determining the exact origin and mechanism of Magic.

Outside of this, Magic and Magic Spells are properly researched and many magic techniques have been discovered over the last 1400 years or so. Mana can be imbued into liquids, metals and crystals for various magical effects -> Potions/Magic Items. And industrial spell casters are utilized to mass produce cheap magical goods in sweatshop or conveyor belt style factories.

# Tech
The main reason why this world underwent a technological/industrial revolution despite the existence of magic is the simple fact that machines are much simpler to mass produce and their production can be fully automated. 

# History
Whatever actually happened under Elysia, it became myth and legend. The Church of Truth ousted the 12th hero as a traitor and immortalized The Nameless Daughter as their Savior, the Holy Lamb. Because of this, the Number 13 in this setting is holy, while the number 12 is seen as the number of misfortune.
